## Risk Mitigation

### Common Failure Modes

**1. Not Enough Pipeline**
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive outbound in months 1-3, build content library before needing clients
- **Backup Plan:** Take 1-2 smaller projects below typical minimums to build momentum

**2. Scope Creep**
- **Mitigation:** Extremely detailed SOWs with explicit deliverables and exclusions
- **Backup Plan:** Change order process for scope additions with additional fees

**3. Client Payment Issues**
- **Mitigation:** 50% deposit before starting, remaining 50% before final delivery
- **Backup Plan:** Friendly collections process, then legal action if necessary (has killed projects before)

**4. Burnout from Too Many Concurrent Projects**
- **Mitigation:** Limit to 4-5 active projects max, stagger start dates
- **Backup Plan:** Bring in trusted contractors for overflow, be willing to decline/defer new work

**5. Economic Downturn Reducing Consulting Spend**
- **Mitigation:** Focus on Tier 2 (LLM safety) which becomes more important in downturn (risk management)
- **Backup Plan:** Pivot to product (course, SaaS tool) for more recession-resistant revenue
